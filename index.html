Choosing a DVR isn't easy, so we decided to compare the most popular models Dish and DirecTV have to offer.

In our research, we wanted to focus on only the most important features: recording capacity, internal storage, simultaneous recording, and "comfort features" (we'll explain this). And because some DVR features rely on specific monthly service plans, we'll provide some insight into what the two companies offer with their DVRs.

DIRECTV DVRs
