Dish vs. DirecTV:
Who's Got the Best DVR?

Choosing a DVR isn't easy, so we decided to compare the most popular models Dish and DirecTV have to offer and make a recommendation.

In our research, we wanted to focus on only the most important features: unit price, recording capacity, internal storage, simultaneous recording, and special features. And becuase some DVR features rely on specific monthly service plans, we'll provide some insight into what the two companies offer with their DVRs.
